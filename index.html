About Me

My name is Mohamed Danialou. I was born in Benin, on the west Coast of Africa. Benin is a French speaking country next to Nigeria. I moved to the UK from France 22 years ago and lived in Milton Keynes near Buckingham. Milton is a town halfway between London and Birmingham and is known for its concrete cows and roundabouts.

Career

I'm a senior cybersecurity consultant currently working for an Oil & Gas company. I have been working in the cybersecurity field for over 22 years, for the most part as a freelance cybersecurity consultant. Working as a freelance cybersecurity consultant has its difficulties but also its perks. It is not always bad. I've lucky to have been to places which otherwise I wouldn't have seen. I also got to meet some very nice people some of whom have become good friends. I've also got to learn a lot and had to keep abreast of new technologies and development by attending conferences, summits and training courses.
